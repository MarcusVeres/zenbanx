Who We Are

ZenBanx was started on the premise that your money is not as free or global as you are.

ZenBanx is a bank account for citizens of the world – those who live, travel, and work in multiple currencies. We are with you everywhere through our simple mobile application and provide access to your money through a multi currency debit card for use at any ATM, anywhere. We liberate your money from costly and hidden fees that other banks typically don’t like to advertise – ours are simple and cheap. We reward your savings with a high competitive interest rate, amongst the highest available.

Our mission is simple: to make your money as mobile as you are.

"Why can’t I exchange money from Canadian to Euro’s in an instant without going to a bank branch?"

— Emma, Toronto

With your ZenBanx account you can hold up to 5 currencies and exchange between them at any time, in real time, in our app.

ZenBanx was started by industry innovators including Arkadi Kuhlmann who was responsible for creating the ING Direct savings revolution in Canada and the United states. Our Chairman Frank Sanchez built the plumbing for most of the banking infrastructure in use today. Our banking partner DUCA is a leader in providing no-nonsense financial services to its customers.
