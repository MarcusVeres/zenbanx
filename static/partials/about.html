Who We Are

ZenBanx Canada was founded in 2014 to introduce Canadians to a simple and secure multi-currency savings account for Citizens of the World. ZenBanx Canada Inc. is a subsidiary of DUCA Financial Services Credit Union Ltd. With offices in Toronto, Silicon Valley, and Delaware, ZenBanx is powered by an experienced banking, foreign exchange, and technology team.
